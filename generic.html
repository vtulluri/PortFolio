About Me

Hi, my name is Vaishnavi Tulluri and I am a Master's student at Arizona State University majoring in Information Technology with a minor in Information Systems Management. Since I was young, I have always enjoyed to solve puzzles and looks things in a broad way.This made me look at big data sets as a one big puzzle I really want to solve.

My personal Projects were built using frameworks in Python such as Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib, Scikit Learn, NLTK, TextBlob and Visualization tools like Tableau, Plotly & PowerBI. Additionally, I have obtained the AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner certification, demonstrating my foundational knowledge of cloud concepts and AWS services. I also earned the Google Advanced Data Analytics certification, which signifies my expertise in utilizing advanced data analytics tools and techniques on the Google Cloud platform. These certifications reflect my commitment to staying up-to-date with the latest cloud technologies and my ability to effectively leverage cloud services for data analytics purposes.

A few interesting facts about me. I love to read novels and I would love to Hike, go on vacations(Current favorite: Telluride, Colorado) and play Throwball. I love learning and I push myself to learn something new everyday, whether that be about data analysis or miscellaneous facts about the universe.
