E-Commerce Churn Prediction

• In the E-Commerce Churn Prediction project conducted in May 2023, I navigated a dataset comprising 24 attributes, encompassing crucial details such as customer ID, churn flag, tenure, preferred device, and city tier. Employing data cleaning techniques, I meticulously prepared the dataset for analysis. Utilizing the OneHotEncoder for feature encoding, I implemented a Decision Tree algorithm that yielded an impressive accuracy rate of 95%. This project not only demonstrated my proficiency in handling and processing diverse datasets but also showcased my ability to leverage machine learning algorithms effectively for predictive analysis. By focusing on churn prediction, the project aimed to provide valuable insights for e-commerce businesses in understanding and mitigating customer attrition, thereby contributing to the enhancement of customer retention strategies in the dynamic e-commerce landscape.

Times Series Analysis on Stock Market

• In this data analytics project, I conducted a thorough examination and visualization of closing price trends, total traded stock volume, and daily percentage returns for key tech industry players, namely Amazon, Google, Apple, and Microsoft. Employing Python's seaborn library, I meticulously visualized inter-relationships between closing prices and daily percentage returns, utilizing advanced multivariable analysis techniques to discern potential correlations and dependencies. Additionally, I executed a Value at Risk (VaR) analysis for each company, assessing potential losses within specified confidence levels. This project yields invaluable insights into the stock market performance of these tech giants, providing essential support for investment decision-making and the formulation of robust risk management strategies.

NBA Players stats since 1950

• The NBA Players Stats since 1950 dashboard is a dynamic and interactive tool that offers a comprehensive analysis of player statistics in the National Basketball Association (NBA) dating back to 1950. Accessible through Kaggle, the dataset includes player attributes such as points per game, rebounds, assists, and field goal percentage. The dashboard employs visualizations like charts and graphs to illustrate historical trends, enable player performance comparisons, and highlight noteworthy records. Tailored for basketball enthusiasts, analysts, and fans, the dashboard provides valuable insights into the rich history of the NBA, allowing users to explore the evolving landscape of player performance over the years.Moreover, it emphasizes data maximization capabilities, empowering users to extract optimal value from the extensive dataset.
